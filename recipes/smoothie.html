Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 200g Greek yogurt
  • Handful of spinach
  • 1 banana (frozen)
  • 75g blueberries (frozen)
  • 50g pineapple chunks (frozen)
  • 10g PB2
  • 7g honey
  • 51g Orgain vanilla protein powder

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to nutribullet
  2. Add water to fill rest of canister
  3. Blend
  4. Enjoy!
